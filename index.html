Ranking Calculator

Wanting an objective way to evaluate my friends' boasts about being the best Smash player, I made a ranking calculator that implements Massey's Ranking method.

Using Matrix operations (written from scratch), Massey's method efficiently considers all match data and spits out ranking scores for each player
