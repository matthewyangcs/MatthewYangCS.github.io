About
Analyzed the socioeconomic impact of the Olympics Games for a week long datathon held in July 2020. Analysis focused primarily on finding significant trends in how the effects of the olympic games dwindle decrease as you get further away from the actual host site. Also examined the empirical effects of the olympic games, such as improving public health.
